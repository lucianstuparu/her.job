# Education & Training

Research, citations, and reflections on peace education, capacity building, and training methodologies for peacebuilding.

## Overview

This area explores educational approaches to peacebuilding, including formal and informal peace education, training design, and capacity building for peace practitioners.

## Key Topics

- Peace education curricula
- Training methodologies and design
- Capacity building approaches
- Transformative learning
- Conflict-sensitive education
- Adult learning principles
- Evaluation of training programs
