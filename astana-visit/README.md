# Astana Field Visit

Materials, plans, and observations for the field research visit to Astana, Kazakhstan.

## Overview

This area contains all materials related to the field visit to Astana, including pre-visit preparation, on-the-ground observations, and post-visit follow-up activities.

## Visit Objectives

- Conduct field research on peacebuilding initiatives in Kazakhstan
- Meet with local stakeholders and practitioners
- Gather insights for CCDP Geneva research project
- Build relationships with local partners
- Assess context and opportunities for peacebuilding work

- Stakeholder meetings and interviews
