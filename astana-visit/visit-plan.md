## Key Questions to Explore

1. What are the main conflict dynamics in the local context?
2. What peacebuilding initiatives currently exist?
3. Who are the key stakeholders and what are their roles?
4. What are the main challenges to peacebuilding work?
5. What opportunities exist for intervention or support?
6. How do gender and inclusion factor into peacebuilding efforts?
7. What role can CCDP Geneva play in supporting local efforts?
