# Conflict Resolution

Research, citations, and reflections on conflict resolution theories, methods, and practices.

## Overview

This area focuses on understanding and addressing conflicts in the Kazakhstan context. It encompasses theoretical frameworks, practical methodologies, and analysis of conflict dynamics specific to peacebuilding work in Kazakhstan.

## Key Topics

- Conflict analysis and assessment
- Conflict transformation vs. conflict resolution
- Third-party intervention
- Negotiation strategies
- Peacebuilding frameworks
- Root causes of conflict
- Conflict escalation and de-escalation
