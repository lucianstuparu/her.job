# Citations - Conflict Resolution

## How to Add Citations

Use this format for each new citation:

```markdown
## Author, Year

**Full Citation:** Author, A. (Year). Title of work. Publisher.

**Key Ideas:**
- Main point 1
- Main point 2

**Relevance:** How this applies to your work

**Tags:** #keyword1 #keyword2
```

---

## Example: Lederach, 1997

**Full Citation:** Lederach, J. P. (1997). Building Peace: Sustainable Reconciliation in Divided Societies. United States Institute of Peace Press.

**Key Ideas:**
- Peacebuilding must be understood as a comprehensive concept
- Importance of middle-range leadership in conflict transformation
- Need for long-term commitment to relationship building

**Relevance:** Foundational text for understanding sustainable peacebuilding approaches

**Tags:** #theory #peacebuilding #reconciliation #leadership
