# Trauma & Healing

Research, citations, and reflections on trauma-informed approaches, psychosocial support, and healing in conflict-affected communities.

## Overview

This area addresses the psychological and emotional impacts of conflict, violence, and displacement. It encompasses trauma theory, healing practices, and trauma-informed peacebuilding interventions.

## Key Topics

- Trauma theory and impacts
- Psychosocial support interventions
- Healing and reconciliation processes
- Trauma-informed practice
- Collective trauma
- Resilience building
- Cultural approaches to healing
