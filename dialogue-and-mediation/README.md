# Dialogue & Mediation

Research, citations, and reflections on dialogue facilitation, mediation processes, and communication in conflict contexts.

## Overview

This area explores the art and science of creating spaces for dialogue and mediating disputes. It includes facilitation techniques, mediation frameworks, and approaches to fostering understanding across differences.

## Key Topics

- Dialogue facilitation methods
- Mediation models and approaches
- Active listening and communication skills
- Third-party neutral roles
- Power dynamics in mediation
- Restorative justice dialogues
- Cross-cultural communication
