# Gender & Inclusion

Research, citations, and reflections on gender perspectives, inclusive practices, and amplifying marginalized voices in peacebuilding.

## Overview

This area examines how gender and inclusion intersect with peacebuilding, ensuring that peace processes and interventions address the needs and perspectives of all community members, particularly those historically marginalized.

## Key Topics

- Gender analysis in conflict and peacebuilding
- Women, Peace and Security (UNSCR 1325)
- Inclusive peacebuilding practices
- Intersectionality in peace work
- Youth inclusion
- LGBTQ+ perspectives in peacebuilding
- Disability-inclusive approaches
